Kirana is an early stage startup striving to develop some interesting products for the benefit of shopkeepers across this country.

Currently, we are pursuing to develop an inventory management android app for a small scale shop.

Our Services

Bar Code Scanning

Items can be scanned by the customer or the shopkeeper and added to cart for a smooth and instant shooping experience.Searching of items based on cateogry, price and various other parameters is also provided.

Invoice generation

Shopkeepers can generate the bill based on customer shopping cart and send a copy of bill to the customer. Customer can choose to pay the bil via majority of options like Cash, Wallet,Netbanking, UPI payment etc.

Inventory Management

Shopkeepers will get periodic updates about stock availability of items. Discounts and offers on based on sales of the product are also suggested by our application. Helps in GST tracking.
